Know Our Gods


Lord Hanuman

Lord Hanuman is a revered deity in Hinduism, Lord Shiv's avatar, known for his unwavering devotion towards Lord Ram, immense strength, celibacy & wisdom.

Lord Ram

Lord Ram, the seventh incarnation of Lord Vishnu, is revered for his unwavering commitment to truth, righteousness, dharma, loyalty and justice .

Lord Shiv

Lord Shiv, also known as Mahadev, is one of the principal deities in Hinduism. The 'Shiv Ling' is a profound symbol depicting Lord Shiv in Hinduism.

Lord Vishnu

Lord Vishnu is one of the principal deities in Hinduism. Lord Vishnu is depicted as a serene and omniscient deity.

Lord Brahma

Lord Brahma is one of the principal deities in Hinduism. He is depicted with four heads, each representing one of the four Vedas.

Lord Krishna

Lord Krishna is one of the most revered deities in Hinduism, worshipped as the eighth avatar of Lord Vishnu. He is renowned for the creation of 'Bhagwat Gita'.

Lord Ganesh

Lord Ganesha is one of the most beloved and widely worshipped deities in Hinduism . He is widely revered as the remover of obstacles and bringer of good luck.

Goddess Durga

Goddess Durga, also known as Shakti, is a principal and powerful deity in Hinduism. She embodies the divine feminine energy and is revered as the protector of the universe.

Goddess Parvati

Goddess Parvati, also known Gauri, is revered as the goddess of power, energy, nourishment, harmony, love, beauty, devotion, and motherhood in Hinduism.

Goddess Lakshmi

Goddess Lakshmi, is one of the principal deities in Hinduism, revered as the goddess of wealth, fortune, prosperity, beauty and happiness.
